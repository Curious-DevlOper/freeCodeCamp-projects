Homayoun Hayati

Through his art, He captures the essence of Iranian culture and presents it in a captivating and thought-provoking manner.

Here's a time line of the artist'S biography:

  • 1985 - Born in Zahedan, Iran
  • 2012 - Leaves his city to attend the Art University of Soore in Tehran.
  • 2016 - Gets graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Art.
  • 2016 - Starts his Collective and Solo Expositions.
  • 2017 - Group Design Exhibition, Haft Samar Gallery, Tehran, Iran.
  • 2018 - “Insomnia”, Art Zone 42 Gallery , Athens, Greece.
  • 2019 - “Unfinished Lines 2”, Muzeu Historik Kombetar, Tirana, Albania.
  • 2020 - Group Exhibition of 100 Work of 100 Artists, Golestan Gallery, Tehran, Iran.
  • 2021 - Solo Exhibition, Saradipour Gallery, Tehran, Iran.
  • 2022 - Solo Exhibition, Haft Samar Gallery, Tehran, Iran.

Hayati's paintings create a new visual atmosphere, blurring the lines between objectivity and abstraction, often revealing narratives in the final stages of his work. Through his art, Hayati captures the essence of Iranian culture and presents it in a captivating and thought-provoking manner.
